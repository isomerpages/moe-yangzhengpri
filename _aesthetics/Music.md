The Music Programme, which includes the&nbsp;**General Music**&nbsp;and&nbsp;**Music Enrichment workshops**&nbsp;(AMIS),&nbsp;**focuses on**&nbsp;nurturing pupils’&nbsp;**creative expression**&nbsp;and&nbsp;**communication through Music.**

  

The&nbsp;**General Music Programme focuses on**&nbsp;nurturing pupils’&nbsp;**creative expression**&nbsp;and&nbsp;**communication through Music**.

  

Pupils will develop an understanding of musical elements and concepts and these are reinforced through musical experiences. These include the following:

  

*   **Imitate rhythmic and melodic patterns**&nbsp;using body percussion, voice and different classroom percussion instruments
*   **Describe the qualities of the music**&nbsp;using appropriate terms when listening and performing music
*   **Perform on a melodic instrument**&nbsp;with good tone and expression
*   **Compose a simple melody**&nbsp;using a range of music instruments
*   **Evaluate and comment on the music**&nbsp;that they have heard
*   **Understand music**&nbsp;in local and global cultures

  

The music curriculum provides opportunities for pupils to&nbsp;**develop a connection to music**&nbsp;and&nbsp;**lay the foundation for a life-long engagement in music**.
